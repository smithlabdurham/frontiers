GEOL2301: Palaeoecosystems

Essay writing assignment

Begin

Formative writing exercise

An opportunity to develop your critical writing skills through practice and feedback

Task

Before week 6, please spend one hour (plus reading time) writing an essay on one of the below questions, drawn from a previous year's examination paper:

  1. How have molecular data improved our understanding of evolutionary processes?
  2. Why don’t molecular clocks recover a “Cambrian explosion” of animal diversity?

Either bring your essay to the classroom session, or submit it by e-mail to martin.smith @durham.ac.uk. Feedback will follow thereafter.
